Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first box?
Tap the "Create Box" button on the home screen, enter a name for your box (like "Kitchen" or "Bedroom 1"), add items to the contents list, and save. A unique QR code will be generated automatically!
How do I print QR code labels?
Open a box, tap the QR code to view it full screen, then use your device's screenshot and print features. You can print it on regular paper and tape it to your box, or use label paper for a cleaner look.
Does Box Buddy work offline?
Yes! Box Buddy works fully offline. You can create, edit, and scan boxes even without WiFi. Your changes will automatically sync when you reconnect to the internet. This is perfect for moving day!
How do I scan a QR code?
You can scan QR codes two ways: (1) Use the built-in scanner in Box Buddy by tapping the scan button, or (2) Use your phone's camera app - most modern phones will recognize QR codes and suggest opening Box Buddy.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
Free: Up to 20 boxes with full offline access, QR codes, and search.
Pro: Unlimited boxes plus any future premium features we add. One-time purchase of $4.99 - no subscription!
Can I share my boxes with family?
[If family accounts available:] Yes! Create a family account and invite up to 4 family members. Everyone can see and edit the shared box inventory.
[If not yet available:] Family sharing is coming soon! For now, you can share screenshots of box contents or let family members use your login temporarily.
How do I upgrade to Pro?
When you try to create your 21st box, you'll see an upgrade prompt. You can also tap "Settings" ‚Üí "Upgrade to Pro" at any time. The purchase is processed through the App Store/Play Store.
Can I export my box list?
Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data. You can download your complete box inventory as a CSV file, which you can open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and stored securely on Google Cloud servers. We never share, sell, or use your box inventory for any purpose other than providing the service. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
What happens if I delete my account?
All your data (boxes, contents, QR codes) will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This cannot be undone. We recommend exporting your data first if you might need it later.
I'm getting sync errors. What should I do?
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Force-close and restart the app
  3. Make sure you're using the latest version
  4. Try logging out and back in
If issues persist, contact support with details about when the error occurs.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google. For iOS: Go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Media & Purchases ‚Üí View Account ‚Üí Purchase History ‚Üí Report a Problem. For Android: Visit the Play Store, find Box Buddy in your purchase history, and request a refund. Refunds are typically only available within 48 hours of purchase.
How do I delete a box?
Open the box, tap the menu icon (three dots), and select "Delete Box." You'll be asked to confirm. Deleted boxes cannot be recovered, so make sure you really want to delete it!
Can I use Box Buddy for storage, not just moving?
Absolutely! Box Buddy works great for storage units, attics, garages, or anywhere you have boxes you want to organize. Many users keep it installed long-term to track their storage.
